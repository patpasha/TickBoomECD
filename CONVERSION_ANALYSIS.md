## 🧠 Psychologie de Conversion Appliquée

### Biais Actuellement Utilisés ✅
1. **Scarcity**: "Only a few spots available" (Lifetime deal)
2. **Social Proof**: "2.5K+ commitments", testimonials
3. **Authority**: "Backed by neuroscience"
4. **Loss Aversion**: "Stop making excuses"
5. **Binary Choice**: "Do or Don't" (force decision)

### Biais Manquants ⚠️
1. **Risk Reversal**: Pas assez de guarantees
2. **Bandwagon Effect**: Manque "Join 1000+ users"
3. **Anchoring**: Pas de comparaison prix (ex: "vs $120/year for competitors")
